I was born in Agrigento, Sicily, in 1988. Since childhood, technology has had a magnetic pull on me. In elementary school I spent hours exploring the web, writing HTML for fun, and learning on my own. By the age of 12 I was publishing small sites about hacking culture on unlikely `.tk` domains, followed by my first real website: **erriko.it**.

Erriko.it was a place where I collected tutorials, tips, and guides for software and devices. Everything I was learning, I documented and shared. Those pages eventually reached hundreds of thousands of yearly visits, and became my first real digital training ground.

I still believe that *the best way to learn is to teach*: explaining things clearly forced me to understand them deeply. Without knowing it, I was an early content creator, publishing articles, Photoshop tutorials, mobile phone modding guides, and receiving feedback, traffic, and my first collaborations in return.

## Early work and first ventures

While attending language high school in the early 2000s, and later studying Economics in Catania, I started freelancing. I built websites for local businesses, logos, small e-commerce projects, and custom digital products. At the time, tools like Shopify and Wix were not part of the mainstream workflow.

By 2008 I was already building working e-commerce systems from scratch, including carts, product management, checkout flows, subscriptions, and payments. I wanted this to become real work, so I opened my own **web agency**. At first I did everything myself, while simulating a larger team through different department email addresses.

In my early twenties I realized that a conventional fixed job was not the right shape for me. My priority was not to find a job, but to **build the work I wanted to do**.

## 2010-2020: ambition, creativity, and startups

My twenties were a period of intense creative energy and experimentation. After freelancing and running my small agency, I moved closer to the startup world, before I fully understood what a startup really was.

Around 2010, my interest in comics and visual storytelling crossed paths with digital product work, and I created **Verticalismi**, an experimental cultural project dedicated to webcomics.

Within a few years, Verticalismi became a reference point in the Italian digital comics scene. In 2014 it won the **Premio Micheluzzi** for Best Webcomic at Napoli Comicon.

In 2015 I turned Verticalismi into **VERTICOMICS**, the first Italian app and marketplace designed around a native digital comics reading experience. We worked with authors, independent publishers, and major brands, raising **almost 500,000 euros from venture capital and business angels**.

Verticomics was accelerated by **LUISS EnLabs**, selected among the **TOP 5 ICT Projects** of the European **ACE Creative Innovation Challenge**, and brought me into the Italian and international startup ecosystem.

Between 2018 and 2019 I worked as **Fractional CTO** for **Yakkyofy**, a B2B dropshipping startup. I coordinated an international team and designed integrations between Shopify, WooCommerce, and a logistics engine for catalogs, orders, fulfillment, and tracking updates.

In 2017 I took part in the **BNP Paribas International Hackathon**, winning the Italian stage with **Mendua**, a B2B marketplace for frictionless leasing, and later reaching the international final.

## 2020 to today: technology, marketing, and new directions

Starting in 2020, I wanted to build something that represented the synthesis of my path: technical depth, strategic vision, and an obsession with efficiency. In 2021 I founded **Traction Group**, a **MarTech company** combining data-driven marketing with proprietary software development.

At Traction I work on project strategy and architecture, but also on **automation, scripting, and customer lifecycle design**. We also built **AutoCust**, a modular system of AI-enhanced marketing tools.

In 2022 I served as **interim CTO** of **MVND Holding**, an Italian foodtech group combining services such as **OffLunch**, **Moovenda**, and **Foodys.it**. I managed the integration of the information systems across brands, improving digital operations and scalability.

In 2025 I collaborated as **Tech Advisor** with **Wash Out**, a scaleup specialized in at-home car washing, designing a logistics optimization algorithm for smarter operator distribution across the territory.

Over the years I have worked with large companies such as **TIM** and **Sparkle**, bringing technology and marketing expertise into enterprise contexts. I also served for several years as a **business coach** for **Boost Your Ideas**, a program promoted by **Lazio Innova S.p.A.**

From 2018 to 2021 I taught in several **training programs for developers and project managers**, covering software design, automation, marketing, and product management.

In 2020 I published an operational Vue.js handbook: **Point of VUE**, available on [Amazon](https://www.amazon.it/Point-VUE-Realizza-applicazione-approccio-ebook/dp/B0846W2FWN).

I enjoy exploring new sectors and becoming, as quickly as possible, expert enough to design a strategy, shape a customer lifecycle, and define meaningful KPIs. Every project forces me to learn, question my tools, and find the most effective way to connect an idea to a result.

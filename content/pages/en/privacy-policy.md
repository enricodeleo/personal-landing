**Last updated: January 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is processed when users visit **enricodeleo.com**.

For specific information about cookies and consent for measurement cookies, see the [Cookie Policy](/en/cookie-policy).

## 1) Data controller

The data controller is:

**Enrico Deleo**  
Email: [privacy@enricodeleo.com](mailto:privacy@enricodeleo.com)

## 2) Types of data processed

### 2.1 Browsing data

During normal browsing, technical data may be processed, including:

- IP address
- request date and time
- requested URL
- browser, operating system, and device information
- logs and diagnostic data

This data is necessary to operate the website, ensure security, and prevent abuse.

### 2.2 Data voluntarily provided by the user

The website may process data provided directly by the user, such as:

- name, if provided
- email address
- phone number, if provided
- content of messages sent by the user

The data is processed only for the purposes described in the "Purposes of processing" section.

### 2.3 Comments through third-party services

The website may use **Disqus**, a commenting service provided by Disqus, Inc. Use of the comment system may involve processing personal data such as IP address, usage data, browser identifiers, and interaction data.

Disqus may also install cookies and tracking technologies, including third-party technologies.

## 3) Purposes and legal bases

### 3.1 Operation, security, and abuse prevention

**Purpose:** ensure website operation, security, and protection from abnormal traffic, attacks, and abuse.

**Legal basis:** legitimate interest of the controller and/or technical necessity to provide the requested service.

### 3.2 User requests and follow-up contact

**Purpose:** respond to requests, questions, or contact requests sent through the AI chat or other channels available on the website.

**Legal basis:** pre-contractual measures or the user's request.

### 3.3 Newsletter

**Purpose:** send informational content, insights, and selected links.

**Legal basis:** user consent.

Commercial or promotional communications are not sent without consent.

### 3.4 Website measurement

**Purpose:** collect aggregated information about website usage to improve content and performance.

**Legal basis:** user consent through the banner or preferences panel.

### 3.5 Comment system

**Purpose:** allow users to comment on website content.

**Legal basis:** user consent expressed through the cookie preferences system.

## 4) AI chat

The website includes an **AI chat** feature. Users may choose to interact with the chat, and the conversation content may be processed to provide the requested answers.

Only when relevant, the chat may ask whether the user wants to be contacted. In that case, the user may choose to provide name, email, and/or phone number.

- Providing contact data is optional.
- Before sending contact details, the chat asks the user to review the Privacy Policy and explicitly confirm acceptance.
- Contact data collected through the chat is used only to manage the contact request and not for profiling.

To support the conversation, the chat may use a temporary session identifier that is technically necessary.

## 5) Newsletter

The website includes a signup form for informational communications. Data provided through the form is collected and processed by the controller through technical automation tools and may then be shared with selected third-party providers for communication management.

- **Data processed:** email address and any other data voluntarily entered by the user.
- **Purpose:** sending informational content and updates.
- **Legal basis:** user consent given at signup.
- **Withdrawal:** users can withdraw consent and unsubscribe at any time through the links included in emails.

## 6) Cookies, preferences, and consent

The website uses cookies or technologies that are:

- **necessary** for operation and always active
- **measurement-related**, such as Google Analytics 4, only after consent

Users can accept or reject measurement cookies through the banner and can change or withdraw their choice through the preferences panel.

For technical details about cookies used, see the Cookie Policy.

## 7) Recipients and providers

Data may be processed by providers acting on behalf of the controller as **data processors** for technical or service purposes.

### 7.1 Google Analytics 4

A web analytics service provided by Google, used exclusively for statistical purposes, without advertising, remarketing, or profiling, and activated only after consent.

Information: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 7.2 Disqus

A commenting service provided by **Disqus, Inc.** Disqus acts as an independent controller for managing comments and user interactions. The service is loaded only after consent.

Information: [https://disqus.com/privacy/](https://disqus.com/privacy/)

### 7.3 Brevo

An email delivery and contact management service.

- **Role:** data processor.
- **Purpose:** managing subscriptions and sending communications.
- Brevo processes data on behalf of the controller according to instructions.

Information: [https://www.brevo.com/legal/privacypolicy/](https://www.brevo.com/legal/privacypolicy/)

### 7.4 Cloudflare

The website uses Cloudflare as a proxy, security, and content delivery service. Cloudflare may process technical data, such as IP addresses and security logs, to ensure performance and protection.

Information: [https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)

### 7.5 Additional technical providers

The website may use additional technical providers, such as hosting, network infrastructure, and technical monitoring tools, exclusively for delivery and security purposes.

## 8) Transfers outside the EU

Some providers may process data outside the European Economic Area. In such cases, the controller adopts appropriate safeguards required by applicable law, such as adequacy decisions or standard contractual clauses.

## 9) Data retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary for the stated purposes, in the controller's technical systems and with third-party providers.

- **Browsing data/logs:** for the time necessary to ensure security and technical management.
- **Contact data collected through chat:** for the time necessary to handle the request and related follow-up.
- **Newsletter data:** until consent is withdrawn, unsubscribe, or deletion is requested.
- **Analytics data:** according to the settings of the services used and only after consent.

## 10) Security measures

The controller adopts reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, such as encrypted connections, security services, abuse prevention, and access controls.

## 11) Data subject rights

Where provided by law, users may exercise rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, objection, portability where applicable, and withdrawal of consent.

To exercise these rights, write to [privacy@enricodeleo.com](mailto:privacy@enricodeleo.com).

### Complaint

Users also have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority. In Italy, this is the **Garante per la protezione dei dati personali**.

## 12) Minors

The website is not intended for minors. If a user believes that data from minors has been submitted, they can contact the controller to request removal.

## 13) Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated over time. Any changes will be published on this page with the updated date.

**Last updated: January 2026**

This Cookie Policy explains how this website uses cookies and similar technologies to ensure proper operation and, with consent, collect aggregated information about website usage.

## What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site work correctly and, in some cases, collect statistical information about usage.

## Types of cookies used

### Necessary cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to work and cannot be disabled. They are used to:

- maintain the browsing session
- allow navigation between pages
- support essential website features, such as the AI chat

### Measurement cookies

These cookies collect aggregated information about website usage to analyze traffic and improve content.

The site may use Google Analytics 4 exclusively for statistical purposes, without advertising, remarketing, or profiling.

These cookies are activated only after consent through the banner or preferences panel.

The site may also use third-party services that install cookies or similar technologies, such as comment systems, loaded only after user consent.

## Managing preferences

You can:

- accept or reject measurement cookies through the first-visit banner
- change or withdraw consent at any time through the preferences panel available on the site
- manage cookies through your browser settings

Disabling necessary cookies may affect website functionality.

## Cookie duration

Session cookies expire when the browser is closed.

Persistent cookies remain stored for a limited period, according to the settings of each service used.

## Changes to this policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated over time. Any changes will be published on this page with the updated date.
